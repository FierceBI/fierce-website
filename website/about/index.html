About Fierce

It started with one idea: the conversation is the relationship.

Susan Scott spent years watching smart organizations fail at the same thing, the conversations they weren't having. Fierce turned that insight into a methodology that's now shaped leaders in more than 100 countries.

Our story

From a bestselling idea to a global standard.

Founder Susan Scott wrote Fierce Conversations after seeing that the success of a leader, and a company, happens one conversation at a time. What began as a book became a methodology, then a company trusted by the world's most demanding organizations.

  • Founded on a single, durable principle
  • Refined over 20+ years of real-world practice
  • Delivered by a global bench of master facilitators
