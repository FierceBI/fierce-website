The program

What you'll learn.

Change is constant, but how you lead through it makes all the difference. Fierce Change equips leaders with the conversation skills to guide teams through disruption with clarity, empathy, and momentum.

  • How to communicate change in a way that builds trust, not fear
  • Techniques for surfacing resistance and turning it into engagement
  • The role of honest conversation in sustaining change momentum
  • How to balance urgency with the patience real transformation requires
  • Building alignment across teams when priorities shift
