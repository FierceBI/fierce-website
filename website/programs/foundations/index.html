The program

What you'll learn.

Fierce Foundations equips your team with the conversation skills to navigate challenges and foster real connections. Every conversation within your team can lead to positive outcomes when you know how to have it.

  • Three transformational ideas for recharging conversations
  • Seven core principles of Fierce Conversations
  • How to identify perceptual filters that shape what you hear
  • The concept of "mokitas," the unspoken truths in your organization
  • Four objectives of authentic conversations
